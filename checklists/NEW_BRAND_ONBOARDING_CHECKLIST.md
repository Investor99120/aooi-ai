# New Brand Onboarding Checklist

Use this checklist when creating a new brand from `templates/brand_template/`.

## Steps

- [ ] Step 1: Create brand folder from template.
- [ ] Step 2: Replace placeholders.
- [ ] Step 3: Complete brand profile.
- [ ] Step 4: Add product facts.
- [ ] Step 5: Separate verified and unverified facts.
- [ ] Step 6: Add claim whitelist.
- [ ] Step 7: Add claim blacklist.
- [ ] Step 8: Add localisation rules.
- [ ] Step 9: Add semantic map.
- [ ] Step 10: Add search intent map.
- [ ] Step 11: Add content cluster map.
- [ ] Step 12: Add FAQ bank.
- [ ] Step 13: Configure publishing targets.
- [ ] Step 14: Confirm `auto_publish_allowed` is `false`.
- [ ] Step 15: Run manual review.
- [ ] Step 16: Mark unresolved fields.
- [ ] Step 17: Approve for engines/adapters only after validation passes.

## Final Decision Values

- `pass` - the brand folder can enter engines and adapters.
- `needs_review` - the brand folder needs human review or source confirmation.
- `blocked` - the brand folder cannot be used by engines or adapters.

## Minimum Approval Requirements

- No required files missing.
- No unresolved customer-facing placeholders.
- No invented product facts.
- No fake reviews or invented proof.
- No risky claims.
- Source status is preserved.
- Manual review completed.
- `auto_publish_allowed` remains `false`.
