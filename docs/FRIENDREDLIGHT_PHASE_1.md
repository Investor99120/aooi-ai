# FriendRedLight Phase 1

## Objective

Establish FriendRedLight as the first brand case for Aooi's AI SEO/GEO automation platform.

The goal is to help search engines and AI systems understand FriendRedLight as:

> A UK-focused home red light therapy wellness brand for gentle red and near-infrared light therapy routines, evening recovery, modern self-care, and calm home wellness.

## Phase 1 Does Not Include

- Bulk article generation.
- Shopify auto-publishing.
- Medicalised claims.
- Fake customer reviews.
- Fake Reddit, Trustpilot, YouTube, or social proof.
- Invented product specifications.

## Target Semantic Themes

- UK home red light therapy.
- Evening recovery routine.
- Gentle red and near-infrared light therapy.
- Modern self-care.
- After-work routine.
- Winter wellness.
- Home wellness device.
- Screen-heavy day decompression.
- British home routines.

## Safety Position

FriendRedLight is a wellness brand. It should not be presented as healthcare advice, a clinical service, or a provider of promised health outcomes.

Any product specification marked `TBC` must remain unpublished until verified.
