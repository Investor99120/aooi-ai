# FriendRedLight Outputs

This folder is the holding area for generated FriendRedLight outputs.

## Purpose

Generated outputs are not live website content. They are reviewable artefacts that can later be copied into Shopify, transformed by an adapter, or revised by a human operator.

## Future Subfolders

- `shopify/` - Shopify-ready page, FAQ, and JSON-LD output.
- `jsonld/` - standalone structured-data files.
- `markdown/` - markdown content briefs and draft pages.
- `audits/` - GEO/SEO, claim, and source-quality reports.

## Rules

- Do not publish automatically.
- Do not include fake reviews.
- Do not include medicalised claims.
- Do not publish unverified product specifications.
- Keep all FriendRedLight customer-facing output in UK English.
