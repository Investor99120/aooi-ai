## What is red light therapy?

Red light therapy is commonly used to describe exposure to visible red light as part of a non-medical wellness routine. This draft should stay educational and avoid healthcare outcome claims.

## Can red light be part of an evening routine?

Red and near-infrared light exposure can be described as part of a calm evening self-care routine, provided the wording avoids promised outcomes and follows product guidance.
