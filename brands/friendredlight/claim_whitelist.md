## Approved Safety Qualifiers

- For home wellness use only.
- Follow the product manual.
- Do not stare directly into LEDs.
- Stop use if discomfort occurs.
- Speak to a qualified professional before use if pregnant, photosensitive, taking photosensitising medication, or managing a medical condition.
- FriendRedLight is not a replacement for qualified professional advice.

## Safer Rewrite Patterns

- Replace "treats pain" with "supports a calming self-care routine".
- Replace "cures insomnia" with "can be used as part of an evening wind-down routine".
- Replace "heals inflammation" with "designed for gentle red and near-infrared light exposure".
- Replace "guaranteed results" with "results may vary depending on consistency and individual use".

## Approved Example Claims

- FriendRedLight is designed for calm home wellness routines.
- Red and near-infrared light exposure can be part of a consistent evening self-care routine.
- The device supports a gentle after-work routine without making healthcare promises.
- FriendRedLight helps create a calmer home wellness moment after screen-heavy days.
- A short, consistent routine may support relaxation and comfort as part of everyday self-care.
- Use FriendRedLight as part of an evening recovery routine, following the product manual.

## Reusable Framework Notes

- Future brand whitelists should keep the same sections: allowed language, positioning, verbs, safety qualifiers, rewrite patterns, and examples.
- Publishing adapters may reuse approved language for page briefs, FAQ drafts, schema descriptions, product-page suggestions, and internal-link recommendations.
