# FriendRedLight UK Localisation

## Language

Use UK English.

Prefer: colour, favourite, centre, cosy, organised, at home, after work, evening routine.

Avoid Americanised or hype-heavy language where possible.

## Lifestyle Context

FriendRedLight should feel natural in UK home routines:

- after the commute
- after a long day at work
- screen-heavy workdays
- closing the laptop
- putting the kettle on
- darker winter evenings
- Sunday reset
- cosy evenings at home
- quiet pre-bed routine
- home office fatigue

## Tone

- Calm.
- Practical.
- Human.
- Trustworthy.
- Understated rather than loud.
- Clear about safety and limitations.

## Avoid

- Aggressive sales language.
- Overly medical language.
- Biohacker-only tone.
- Cheap marketplace tone.
- Keyword stuffing.
- Generic AI-sounding wellness copy.
